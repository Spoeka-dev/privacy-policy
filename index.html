02

Data Storage & What We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our own servers.

All data you enter — including your name, email address, shift times, and calendar entries — is stored exclusively in one of two places, both under your full control:

  • Locally on your device – Stored in the app's private storage on your Android device, never accessible to us or any third party.
  • Your personal Google Drive – If you enable the optional backup feature, data is saved directly to your own Google Drive. We have no access to it at any time.

Notifications (e.g. shift reminders) are generated entirely on-device using Android's local notification system. No data is sent to any external service to deliver them.

03

Google Drive Backup (Optional)

The backup feature is entirely manual and user-initiated. Data is only written to your Google Drive when you explicitly trigger a backup within the app — no automatic or background sync takes place. The feature uses Google's Drive API to write and read files exclusively within a dedicated folder in your personal Google Drive. This means:

  • Only you (and Google, as the platform operator) can access your backup files.
  • We never see, access, or process the contents of your Google Drive.
  • You can revoke the app's Drive access at any time via your Google Account settings.
  • Deleting the app does not automatically delete backup files — manage them directly in Google Drive.

When using Google Drive, Google's own privacy policy applies. See policies.google.com/privacy.

04

Legal Basis for Processing

Since we do not process your personal data on our own infrastructure, there is no data processing by us requiring a legal basis under Art. 6 GDPR. All data operations occur locally on your device or within your own Google account.

The only exception is if you contact us directly (e.g. for support). In that case, we process your contact details based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR and delete them once the matter is resolved.

05

Portability (Art. 20)

Objection (Art. 21)

Complaint (Art. 77)
