Section 02

Local Data Handling

Multishift is designed to function without a central backend.

All personal data, including shift schedules, financial settings, and notes, is stored locally in your app's private SQLite database. We never have access to this information.

  • Calendar Permission: The app reads your system calendar locally to merge your personal events into the shift view. This data is never stored by us.
  • Holiday Sync: We use the Nager.Date API to provide holiday information. No personal identifiers are transmitted during this request.

Section 03

Encrypted Backups (Google Drive)

Backups are user-initiated and secure. We implement the following protections:

AES-256 GCM Encryption: Your backup file is encrypted on your device using a key unique to you before it is uploaded. Only your Recovery Key can unlock it.

  • User Responsibility: You must save your Recovery Key. We cannot reset or recover this key for you.
  • Limited Scope: We only request access to the "App Data" folder on your Drive, ensuring we cannot see your other personal files.

Section 04

Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the full right to access and control your data. Multishift makes this easy through built-in features:

Data Portability

Export all your data into a standard CSV file at any time via the settings.

Right to Erasure

Use the "Reset App" feature to permanently delete all local databases and keys.

Consent Revocation

Disconnect your Google Drive account at any time to stop cloud features.
